## Voice rules (Uzbek)

- No translated marketing speak ("amaliy yechimlar", "qayta tasavvur qilish") — use concrete, conversational Uzbek
- Lead with relatable analogies (bankomat, LEGO, blanka) before any abstract concept
- Numbers, time, cost = concrete (e.g. "Oylar – yillar", not "uzoq vaqt")
- Verbs in active form (qiladi, bajaradi, yordam beradi) not passive
- Address the audience directly when natural ("siz", "xodim", "bank")
